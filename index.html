Viswanathan Anand

A Chess Player

Viswanathan Anand at Sinquefield Cup in 2016.

Here's a timeline of Viswanathan Anand:

  • 1969 - Anand was born at Chennai , Tamil Nadu on 11th December.
  • 1983 - Anand at the age of fourteen won the National Sub-junior Chess Championship.
  • 1984-1985 - Anand at the age of fifteen became the youngest Indian to achieve the title of International Master by winning the Asian Junior Championship for the two consecutive years.
  • 1987 - at the age of eighteen, he became the first Indian to win the 'World Junior Chess Championship'.
  • 1988 - Anand became India's first 'Grandmaster' by winning the Shakti Finance International Chess Tournament.
  • 2000 - Anand won the FIDE World Chess Championship which was a 100-player single-elimination tournament held in New Delhi, India and Tehran, Iran.
  • 2007 - Anand wins World Chess Championship 2007 held in Mexico City against Russian Grandmaster Vladimir Kramnik.
  • 2008 - Anand convincingly defended the title against Kramnik in Bonn, Germany.
  • 2010 - Anand plays his fourth Workd Chess Championship match agaist Bulgerian Grandmaster Veselin Topalov.
  • 2012 - Anand plays agaist Israeli Grandmaster Boris Gelfand for Workd Chess Championship match in Tretyakov Gallery, Moscow and wins it for fifth time.
